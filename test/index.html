ABOUT US

Gospel Light int — Fruitful Youth

The church is situated at the Nungua beach drive, right behind, Liberty Industries Co. Ltd

The church was founded by the Preciding Bishop Mathew Addae Mensah and his wife, Lady Bishop Addae

Gospel light was founded in the year 1992 by the Preciding beshop and his wife following a call to redeem Gods children through deliverance.

OUR MISSION

Our Mission is to spread the Gospel accross Ghana and beyond, and set captives free through deliverance
